Data Deletion Request

Last updated: February 2026

Your Right to Data Deletion

In accordance with data protection regulations (GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws), you have the right to request the deletion of your data from our systems.

What Data Can Be Deleted

Upon request, we can delete the following data associated with your accounts:

  • Advertising performance data (campaign metrics, spend, conversions)
  • Analytics data collected from your properties
  • CRM data synced from connected systems
  • Account identifiers and API connection information
  • Historical reports and dashboards

How to Request Data Deletion

Option 1: Email Request

  1. Send an email to gabriel.garcia@grupofahrenheit.com
  2. Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include:
    • Your name and company
    • Ad Account ID(s) or Business Manager ID
    • Email associated with the account
    • Confirmation that you are authorized to make this request

Option 2: Contact Form

Processing Timeline

  • Acknowledgment: Within 48 hours of receiving your request
  • Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity (1-3 business days)
  • Deletion: Data will be deleted within 30 days of verification
  • Confirmation: You will receive confirmation once deletion is complete
Important Note: Deleting your data will remove all historical reports and analytics associated with your accounts. This action cannot be undone. Any active integrations will stop functioning once data is deleted.

Data Retention After Deletion

After processing your deletion request:

  • All personal and account data will be permanently removed from our active systems
  • Backup systems will purge the data within 90 days
  • Aggregated, anonymized statistics may be retained for internal analytics

Exceptions

We may retain certain data if required by law, for legitimate business purposes (such as fraud prevention), or to resolve disputes. We will inform you if any exceptions apply to your request.
