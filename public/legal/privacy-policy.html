Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Reset Agencia de Medios ("Reset", "we", "us", or "our") operates the Reset Algorithm platform, a marketing intelligence and analytics tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our application and services.

2. Information We Collect

Our application collects and processes the following types of data:

  • Advertising Performance Data: Campaign metrics, spend, impressions, clicks, conversions, and other performance indicators from connected ad accounts (Meta Ads, Google Ads).
  • Analytics Data: Website traffic and user behavior data from Google Analytics 4.
  • CRM Data: Lead and contact information from connected CRM systems (HubSpot).
  • Business Account Information: Ad account IDs, page IDs, and business manager information necessary for API access.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Generate marketing performance reports and dashboards
  • Analyze campaign effectiveness and ROI
  • Provide budget optimization recommendations
  • Track cost-per-lead (CPL) and cost-per-acquisition (CPA) metrics
  • Create unified cross-channel marketing analytics

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data:

  • All API credentials are stored encrypted
  • Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • We use secure HTTPS connections for all data transfers
  • Data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share aggregated, non-personally identifiable information with clients for reporting purposes.

6. Data Retention

We retain marketing performance data for up to 2 years for historical analysis and reporting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 8).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about your accounts
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format

8. Data Deletion Requests

To request deletion of your data, please visit our Data Deletion Page or contact us directly at the email below.

9. Third-Party Services

Our application integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Marketing API
  • Google Ads API
  • Google Analytics 4 API
  • HubSpot API

Each of these services has their own privacy policies that govern their data practices.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
